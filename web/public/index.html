Step 3

The ad

Pick a placement, then upload your artwork. Use the matching PowerPoint template to nail the size.

⚠️

Dark or black backgrounds only. The scoreboard is a high-brightness LED wall — white or white-dominated artwork can overdrive and damage its LEDs, so the facility requires a dark background with light-colored text. Ads on a white background will be rejected.

Full-color PNG or JPG, up to 50 MB. The exact size & aspect are checked after upload.

Make it read on the board

  • ✓Full color on a black or very dark background.
  • ✓Bold block (sans-serif) type — script and serif fonts blur on the board.
  • ✓Few words. Don't use eight words when four will do.
  • ✕No white or light backgrounds — they'll be rejected.
